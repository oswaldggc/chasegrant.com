About

Who is Chase Grant?

Chase Grant is a lawn care business owner, landscaping entrepreneur, and content creator based in Trinity, Florida. He's the founder of Grow Landscaping, a lawn care and landscape company he's building with one goal: breaking into the top 100 landscaping companies in the world.

Along the way, Chase documents the entire process — the wins, the mistakes, the numbers, and the systems — to help other owners and operators grow their own lawn care and landscaping businesses. From route density and pricing to hiring crews and scaling recurring revenue, he shows the ins and outs that most companies keep behind closed doors.
